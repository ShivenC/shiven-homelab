Why I built this

Reading about an attack and detecting one are different skills. I built this lab to live on the defender’s side of that gap — to generate a real Kerberoasting attempt or credential dump, watch it surface in the logs, and write the detection that catches it next time. It’s the closest thing to a real SOC I can run on a laptop, and every phase is documented here so the work speaks for itself.
